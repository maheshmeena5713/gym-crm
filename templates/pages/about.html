Our Story

Founded in 2024, GymEdge started with a simple observation: gym owners were spending more time wrestling with spreadsheets than helping their members get fit.

Managing a gym involves complex logistics—billing, attendance, staff management, and member retention. Most software solutions were clunky, outdated, or disconnected.

We built GymEdge to change that. By combining powerful CRM features with predictive AI, we help gym owners automate the mundane and focus on what matters most: community and growth.
